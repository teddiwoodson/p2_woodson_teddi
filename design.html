About Me

Thanks for dropping by! My name is Teddi and ever since I can remember, I've had a passion for storytelling. In hopes of turning that passion into a profession, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Public Relations from the University of Florida in the spring of 2019.

Four years, 2,368 cups of coffee, and one degree later, I was still yearning for more. So, I decided to continue my education and pursue a Master's degree in Mass Communication with a Specialization in Web Design. Long story short, my goal is to continue telling stories through the creation of written, graphic and web-based content in the digital marketing sector.

To see more of what I do, what I've done, or to get in touch, check out my other pages!
